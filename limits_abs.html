Limits With Absolute Value

Limits with Absolute Value requires the use of logic. We know that no matter what we input the value within the absolute signs will always be positive. Thus, if the limit is from the right, then you can remove the abs sign as it wont be negative because the right side is greater than C. However, if its from the left side then the asnwer within the abs will be negative because the left side of C is less than C. So, we instead take the negatrive out along with the abs symbol.
