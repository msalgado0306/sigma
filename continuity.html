Continuity

By definition Continuity in Calculus means that there is no break in the functions graph. If a function is not continuous then it can fit into one of two categories of discontinuity, which are: Removable and Nonremovable.

A Removable discontinuity means that...

---The limit x->c f(x) does exist

--- Doesnt matter if f(c) is defined
